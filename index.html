h3 What is Kingdom Builders?

Kingdom Builders is a Radiant Church strategic initiative focused on the local and global expansion of outreach, evangelism, and mercy ministry.

Whether it be sending missionaries to the nations, planting new churches, or caring for the poor, we believe that as Christians we are called to participate actively in the Great Commission by impacting the world and building the Kingdom of God in every sphere of society.

h3 Give

We have two primary ways for you to financially resource Kingdom Builders:

  • Our annual Big Give Offering. This special annual offering serves as the first-fruits of our community’s investment in all of our Kingdom Builders initiatives.
  • Ongoing financial partnership. Every time you give, you have the opportunity to designate a specific amount of financial support directly to Kingdom Builders. These funds will be used solely to resource all of our Kingdom Builders initiatives.

Take a look at our Global Outreach page for a description of our giving strategy to missions all over the world.

Give Now

You can also give by check: Make your check out to Radiant Church and note Kingdom Builders. Mail to the Finance Department at 8157 East DE Ave, Richland, MI 49083.

h4 Xplore Groups

Together, we'll be studying Xplore from the Center for Mission Mobilization. Xplore is a 7-lesson study to help believers discover the biblical theme of God's global purpose, the task remaining in frontier missions, major world religions, and how they can participate in God's global purpose, no matter their location or vocation.

h1 Get Involved at Radiant

If you’re new to Radiant or consider Radiant your home church and want to get more involved, then the Be Radiant class is the next step for you!

The Be Radiant Class is designed to help you connect to the vision, purpose, and heartbeat of Radiant Church. In this class hosted by our campus pastors, you’ll get to hear from Pastor Lee, get to know Radiant better, and learn how you can get more involved.
